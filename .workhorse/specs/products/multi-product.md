Workhorse manages specs across multiple products. Products are the user-facing concept (Tamanu, Tupaia) — each product maps to a GitHub repository under the hood.

## Multi-product

- [ ] Products can be added by giving them a name and linking a GitHub repository URL
- [ ] Each team belongs to a single product
- [ ] A feature card's product is determined by its team
- [ ] The AI interviewer loads context from the team's product codebase
- [ ] Specs are committed to the team's product codebase
- [ ] One feature always targets exactly one product
- [ ] Product management is in settings
- [ ] Products appear in sidebar navigation, linking to spec explorer and feature cards
