Row 1 — hover state

X button visible on hover

The dismiss button appears on row hover. A gradient fades from transparent to the row's current background colour (sidebar, hover, or active surface) over 16px, masking any truncated text cleanly.

Gradient backgrounds

Three background states

At rest: fades to #f1efeb (sidebar bg)
On hover: fades to #edeae5 (hover bg)
Active item: fades to #ffffff (surface bg)

The X button itself gets hover:bg-[--bg-hover] for its own hit-state affordance.

Long titles

Truncation + fade

Row 5 shows a long title. The text truncates at the row edge; the gradient then provides a soft visual boundary before the X appears. No text bleeds under the button.
