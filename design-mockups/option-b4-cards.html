I've reviewed the Tamanu codebase. Let's develop the acceptance criteria for AI-driven spec interview.

The monorepo has 18 packages — React and Material-UI on the frontend, Express and Sequelize on the backend, with multi-directional sync between facilities. A couple of questions to start with:

  1. Should the interview happen within Workhorse's UI, or should it also work as a standalone CLI tool?
  2. When the AI identifies an interaction with existing functionality — say a new medication field affecting patient merge logic — how should that be surfaced? As a warning, or as a required decision point?
